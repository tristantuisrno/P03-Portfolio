Hi, my name is Tristan


...and I am currently an undergraduate student, specializing in UX/UI design and pursuing a minor in Business Administration at Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts and Technology.


Having been a content creator in YouTube's partner program at a young age, I naturally had a fascination for producing and marketing videos. Being self-taught for over seven years in video editing and graphic design, I found myself experimenting most with storytelling and creating graphics to best engage audiences.


Upon joining SFU's SIAT program, I found that aside from being strictly used for advertising, design can be a way of communication and aiding audiences in achieving particular goals. With skills in UX/UI design, graphic design, and motion graphics I seek to understand users and their complexities to innovate, inspire, and problem-solve.
